3️⃣ Kubeflow-Specific Guidance (Important)

Linux Foundation does not certify Kubeflow directly, and that’s actually a good thing.

Your recommended framing:

Kubeflow is a platform responsibility, not a “data science toy”

Students should demonstrate:

Kubeflow pipelines
Identity-bound access to experiments
Artifact storage discipline
Failure recovery (not just success paths)

Certs that indirectly support Kubeflow

CKA (cluster ops)
CKS (runtime security)
Cloud platform certs (AWS/GCP) — secondary

Kubeflow without Kubernetes discipline produces dangerous engineers.
Kubernetes discipline without Kubeflow limits modern relevance.
You are teaching the intersection.
